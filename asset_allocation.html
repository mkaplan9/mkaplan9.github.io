Asset Allocation Simulation

This tool allows you to compare how different asset allocations would have done over time. For now, the options of the tool are stocks, represented by the S&P 500 on January 1st of each year, and bonds, represented by the average yield on 10-Year Treasuries for a given year. Neither is a perfect metric, but they're both decent proxies.
